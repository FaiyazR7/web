Yoyos are something we all know about. Well, they have existed for literal millenias as they were originally invented in ancient Greece, making them one of the oldest toys in existence. They have been popular in many regions of the world for a very long period of time but in the recent decades becuase of industrialization, everyone started playing with yoyos.
